A Mere Sampling of the Full Catastrophe

What you're witnessing above is but a tiny morsel from the grand buffet of blunders. Public sources document tens of thousands of false or misleading statements and hundreds of failures, scandals, and controversial actions by Trump.

Care for the shocking numbers?

The Washington Post alone counted a staggering 30,573 false or misleading claims during his first term—that's approximately 21 fibs per day, which rather puts one's occasional "I'll just have one biscuit" to shame.

Where might one find the complete catalogue?

There are entire Wikipedia pages, congressional trackers, and news archives dedicated to these failures and controversies—enough reading material to last you through several British winters, numerous cups of tea, and possibly the collapse of the Commonwealth.

We do apologize for the brevity. One can only document so much absurdity before requiring a proper cup of tea and a lie-down.
